Phool

Game Manual

Controls: W A S D + Mouse, Left Click to shoot, hold TAB to see your level and upgrades progression

Objective: Collect orbs and kill enemies to get experience and level up

The in-game level is different from the player lvl, only a bit of the experience that you'll collect in each run will be permanent.

This game is simple!

Pick up the exp orbs

while running from your enemies, you can shoot them down but if they reach you one life will be lost

Level up and upgrade your powers

If you get hit or level up you'll get a shield that will protect you for a short amount of time.

The more experienced and powerful you get, the worse your enemies will be!

Upgrades

"Extra Life"

Get one more extra life

"Accuracy"

Improve the precision of your shooting

"Rate of Fire"

Increments the rate of fire of your weapon

"Bullet Splitter"

+1% chance of shooting more than one bullet at once (max 6 bullets)

"Explosive Bullets"

+1% chance of a bullet exploding when an enemy is hit

"Heavier Bullets"

Your bullets will do more damage

"Orb Magnet"

Increase the radius of your orb magnet

"Faster Boots"

Increase your movement speed
